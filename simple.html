Craps (Game)

Rules:

Win    ›  7 or 11 on the first roll or continue to roll until the point is reached.
Lose   ›  2, 3 or 12 on the first throw. Or by rolling a 7 before making the point.
Point  ›  4, 5, 6, 8, 9 or 10 on first throw.
Roll the dice to start playing !
